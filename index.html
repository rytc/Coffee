Coffee is Flavorful!

Pour over coffee is the best way to prepare your morning coffee. This method of preparing coffee really brings out the subtle flavors of fruity and nutty flavors commonly found in high quality coffee.

The Costa Rica Pura Vida coffee from Bodhi Leaf Coffee Traders features notes of cherry, raspberry and milk chocolate. Pour over coffee is the best way to enjoy these subtle flavores.

The other benefit to this brewing method is the reduction in bitterness compared to other home brewing methods

How to Prepare

  1. Ground your roasted beans at a medium grind. While you can use pre-ground coffee, it is better to grind the beans yourself to make sure you get the best ground size
  2. Put 10 grams of coffee grounds for each 8oz of water.
  3. Boil water in a kettle
  4. After the water boils, let it cool for a few seconds for it to stop bubbling
  5. Slowly pour the water over the grounds, making sure all of the coffee grounds soak in water
  6. Once coffee is brewed, enjoy your cup of joe!
